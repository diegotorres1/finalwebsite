Renewable Energy Device

This self adjusting solar panel, reorients itself according to the location of the incoming light to achieve maximum photovoltaic operation. The Arduino starter kit was used to achieve the 1-axial movement. Light dependent resistors were used to measure the intensity of the light to self correct the panel.
